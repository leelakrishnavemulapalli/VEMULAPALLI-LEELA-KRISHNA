About Me

I am Sarvani Nalluru, an accomplished dancer with expertise in various dance forms, especially Kuchipudi. Currently, I am pursuing a B.Tech degree at Malineni Lakshmaiah Women's Engineering College. My journey in the world of dance has been enriched by the training I received under Kuchipudi dancer Vedantam Durga Bhavani Garu.

Achievements

  • Performed in Sankranti Sambaralu and Samskruti events at my college.
  • Won first prize at the VIVA program conducted by VVIT College.
  • Successfully completed my first year of B.Tech with distinction.

Education

  • Completed 12th class with excellent grades.
  • Diploma in Satvikabhinayam, a form of Kuchipudi.
  • Enrolled in a diploma in Yakshaganam.
  • Currently pursuing a B.Tech degree at Malineni Lakshmaiah Women's Engineering College.

Training

I have been trained in Kuchipudi under the guidance of the renowned dancer Vedantam Durga Bhavani. My training has instilled in me a deep appreciation for the art and has allowed me to excel in my performances.
